Brands We Sell & Install

Aciq & Tempstar — equipment we stand behind.

We carry two brands because we'd rather know two lines deeply than stock a dozen and recommend whichever one we have. Both are reliable, well-supported, and a good value for West TN homes.

Brand 01

Aciq

Aciq makes affordable, high-efficiency heat pumps, air handlers, and gas furnaces. They're a great choice when you want strong performance without the premium-brand price tag.

Why we install Aciq

We're a Bronze Extended Dealer — instead of the standard 7-year parts warranty, you get 10 years on parts and 12 years on the compressor.

  • 10-year parts warranty & 12-year compressor warranty (Bronze Extended Dealer)
  • Self-cleaning units
  • Inverter-driven heat pumps with strong cold-weather performance
  • Quiet outdoor units
  • Most of these systems qualify for significant TVA discounts — ranging from $250 to $800

Brand 02

Tempstar

Tempstar is a long-standing American HVAC brand built for the long haul — engineered to handle Tennessee summers and the cold snaps we get every winter.

Why we install Tempstar

  • Lifetime HVAC compressor warranty options
  • Wide range of SEER2 ratings, including TVA-eligible models
  • Easy parts availability across the Mid-South
  • Backed by one of the largest HVAC manufacturers in North America

Filters

Aprilaire whole-home filters.

A cheap fiberglass filter protects the equipment — barely. An Aprilaire media filter actually cleans the air your family breathes. We supply, install, and stock replacements for Aprilaire across West TN.

Why Aprilaire

Better filtration, fewer replacements, cleaner coils.

Aprilaire media filters trap a lot more than a standard 1" fiberglass filter — and they only need to be changed once or twice a year instead of every month.

Whole-Home Media Filters

4" and 5" thick pleated media — captures dust, pollen, pet dander, and microscopic particles your old filter misses.

Lasts 6–12 Months

No more monthly trips to the store. One Aprilaire filter lasts up to a year depending on the model and household.

Protects Your Equipment

Cleaner air = cleaner coils, longer compressor life, and lower energy bills. Pays for itself over time.

Models we install

Common Aprilaire models for West TN homes.

Aprilaire 213

MERV 13 — captures airborne viruses, bacteria, smoke, and fine dust. A solid all-around upgrade for most homes.

Aprilaire 210

MERV 11 — strong filtration for allergy and asthma sufferers without restricting airflow.

Aprilaire 413 / 410

Deep 4" media filters for the larger Aprilaire cabinets — long life, minimal pressure drop.

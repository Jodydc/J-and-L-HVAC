01 — Installation

HVAC Installation & Replacement

New homes, replacements, full system changeouts. We sell and install Aciq and Tempstar equipment — heat pumps, gas furnaces, air handlers, mini-splits, and matched coil/condenser systems.

02 — Service

Service, Cleaning & Tune-Ups

Most breakdowns are preventable. A spring or fall tune-up catches refrigerant issues, dirty coils, weak capacitors, and a clogged condensate line before they leave you sitting in 95° heat.

  • Seasonal tune-ups (heating & cooling)
  • Coil cleaning — indoor & outdoor
  • Refrigerant top-off & leak diagnosis
  • Same-day & next-day repair across West TN
  • $50 TVA rebate available on cleaning

Custom Ductwork — Fabricated On-Site

We don't pull pre-made trunks off a shelf. We fab metal ductwork at the job, sized to your home's actual load — tight seams, fewer transitions, better airflow.

  • Sheet-metal fabrication on the job site
  • New construction & remodels
  • Replacement of old or undersized duct runs
  • Sealed and insulated to code

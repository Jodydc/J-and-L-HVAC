About Us

Locally owned. Fully licensed. Built on doing the work right.

J & L Heating & Cooling LLC is a family-run heating and cooling contractor serving West Tennessee — Jackson, Dyersburg, Brownsville, Maury City, Alamo, Bells, Friendship and Gadsden.

Our story

One licensed crew — so the job actually gets finished.

We started J & L Heating & Cooling LLC because we kept watching homeowners get bounced between trucks. Same crew that sizes your system also installs it and runs the ductwork.

We're CMC-C licensed for HVAC and TVA Approved. And we fabricate our own ductwork on-site, so every install is sized right for your house.

We live and work in West Tennessee. When you call us, you reach Jaden or Lexi — not a call center.

Credentials

What "fully licensed" actually means.

Every license we hold is current, in Tennessee, and in-house. You're not paying for a sub-contractor's markup.
